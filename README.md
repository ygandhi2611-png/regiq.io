# RegIQ — Regulatory & Quality Intelligence

> Free AI tool for regulatory pathway classification and QA readiness scoring — covering every regulated health product, across every major global authority.

🔗 **[Live Tool → regiq.com](https://yashgandhi26.github.io/regiq)**

---

## What it does

RegIQ is an AI-powered tool that helps life sciences professionals answer two of the most critical early-stage questions in regulated product development:

**1. Regulatory Pathway (RA Module)**
Describe your product in plain English → RegIQ identifies your product classification, FDA center assignment, submission type, global pathways, and gives you a regulatory risk score (1–100) with a full breakdown — all cited to real FDA, EMA, and ICH guidance documents.

**2. QA Readiness (QA Module)**
Describe your product and current QMS situation → RegIQ scores your Quality Management System readiness against the right standard (ISO 13485, 21 CFR 820, ICH Q10, EU MDR), identifies your top gaps, and flags the deficiencies most likely to appear in an FDA inspection or ISO audit.

## What you get

### RA — Regulatory Pathway
- Product classification (drug / device / biologic / combination product)
- FDA center assignment — CDER, CDRH, or CBER — with justification
- Submission type — 510(k), PMA, De Novo, NDA, BLA, IND, MAA, and more
- Global pathways across 7 authorities
- Regulatory risk score 1–100 with 4-dimension breakdown
- Top 3 regulatory risks with exact CFR / ICH / EU MDR citations
- Direct links to primary regulatory sources for verification

### QA — Readiness Score
- QMS standard mapping — ISO 13485, 21 CFR Part 820, ICH Q10, EU MDR QMS
- Readiness score 1–100 with element-level breakdown
- Gap analysis across document control, CAPA, supplier controls, design controls, training, audit readiness
- Top deficiency flags modelled on real FDA 483 observations
- Applicable GxP requirements (GMP, GDP, GCP, GLP) by product and market
- Direct links to FDA, ISO, and EMA quality system guidance
